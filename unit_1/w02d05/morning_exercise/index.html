age responsive web designs
Different age group of users react differently to a myriad of content, layout, and aesthetics. Vary your customer's experience through their perceived age with the use of META data for better customer connection.
skeleton screens
Webpage load time contributes unimaginably to the user experience. For pages with varid data formats, it is wise to load your pages in sections. Starting from the simple to more complex, so that customers could anticipate what data they are going to see next. This keeps the bounce rate at a bare minimum.
