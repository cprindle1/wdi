Age Responsive Web Designs

Different age group of users react differently to the myriad of content, layout and aesthetics. Vary your customer's experience through their perceived age with the use of META data for better customer connection

Skeleton Screens

Webpage load time contributes unimaginably to the user experience. For pages with varied data formats, it is wise to load your page in sections. Starting from the simple to the more complex, so that customers could anticipate what data they are going to see next. This keeps the bounce rate at a bare minimum.

Engagement Bots

Chat bots are not a new phenomenon for businesses, but now, they are used in new ways to help stream line the sales process so that your website keeps perofrming round the clock

Upsell Marketing

Upselling to an existing customer is easier than upselling by offering bundle discounts on check-out stage.

Pro-tip

You can increase your yearly revenue by 30% by offering upsell discounts!

Animated Call-to-Action (CTA) Buttons

Ideally and un-sung hero of any webpage, CTA attract the users's attention and induce them to take action. By adding a subtle movement to your CTA buttons you can send polite triggers for action to your online visitors, periodically.

Real-time Persuader Videos

Gone are the days when brands used animated videos to persuade prospects to make an online sale. Today, they do it through real-time videos that feature real people either sharing their experinces with the product or giving a live demo. With real people having to vouch for their products, brands are actually making a quick connection and are making theri way towards a sale, smoother.

Landing Page First

In 2017, expect a devaluation of the home page, which is to give way to the rise of the user specific landing pages. This essentially means there will be variants of your home page targeting users according to their requirements. By delivering specific messages to specific audience, it will get much easier to send the sales pitch across.
